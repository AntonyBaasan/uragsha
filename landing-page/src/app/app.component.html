Uragsha application will help you find workout buddy. Never workout alone.

1. Schedule a session

Select a date and time when you want to exercise. We will send an email to confirm your session once we match a person who wants to do a workout at same time.

2. Start your video session

Each workout session lasts 15 min. You great your partner and add your goals to the exercise list or pull from the previously created exercise list.

3. End your session

Once you are done with the workout, leave a message for the partner. Human accountability will help you both achieve a great result.
